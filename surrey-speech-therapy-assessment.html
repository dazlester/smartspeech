Are You Concerned About Your Child's Speech?

Having a child who is struggling with their speech can be a very stressful experience - for both you and the child.

As a parent, carer or guardian, you want to know exactly what is wrong and how you can help.

But long waiting lists, the inconvenience of travel and appointment times, and high private therapy fees make accessing the expertise you need even more difficult, increasing your stress levels.

To get you the help you need, SmartSpeech provides an online speech assessment process, giving you and your child quick, easy and affordable access to a qualified and experienced Speech and Language Therapist.

So to put your mind at ease, get started today by filling in our free online questionnaire, which will allow us to check whether online speech assessment is suitable for you and your child.

How Does it Work?

Have Your Child Assessed in 3 Easy Steps

Step 1 - Complete our free online questionnaire

This will allow us to assess whether your child is suitable for online assessment (if not, we'll let you know why and give you some advice on what you should do instead).

Step 2 - Upload a short video of your child's speech

We'll introduce you to your fully qualified speech and language therapist, and ask you to upload a short video recording, using our simple assessment guide & instructions.

Step 3 - Receive your speech assessment report

Your child will be assessed, following standard practice guidelines, and a report will be provided by email from your speech and language therapist within 24hrs.

100% Safe & Secure

All videos uploaded to SmartSpeech are stored in secure, private and fully-encrypted cloud-based folders, accessible only by you and your assigned Speech Therapist.


All information is treated with patient confidentiality, and will not be shared with anyone else. We will store information for up to 12 months before it is deleted (unless you request otherwise), so that we have your child’s information if you decide to use SmartSpeech again in the future for a review assessment.

Who Carries Out the Assessment?

Your child's online speech assessment will be carried out by a fully qualified, experienced and highly trained Speech & Language Therapist.

  • Licensed to practice through the Health and Care Professions Council

  • Members of the Royal College of Speech and Language Therapists

  • Specialising in Child Speech Sound Development
