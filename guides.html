Questions?

I'm not sure which guide I need?

If you are unsure fill in our online assessment form. We'll then get in touch with you to arrange a free, no obligations consultation call with your appointed speech therapist. At this stage, we will be able to advise you on your options and answer any questions you have.

What if I buy the wrong guide?

If you purchase a guide and you think it might be the wrong one, let us know within 24hrs and we'll swap it for one which is better suited.

How do I pay for SmartSpeech services?

You can make a secure online payment using all major credit and debit cards.
